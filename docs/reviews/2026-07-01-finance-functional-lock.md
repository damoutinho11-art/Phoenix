# Finance Functional Lock

**Date:** 2026-07-01
**Status:** Locked for this phase

## Verified Functional Scope

- Current portfolio state is source-backed and price refreshable.
- The approved weekly brief closes the recommendation window until the next ISO week.
- Manual checklist, brief history, research audit, and candidate resolution remain backend-driven.
- Performance history renders only real SQLite snapshots and reports an honest empty/insufficient state.
- Finance scrolling, bottom-navigation clearance, and responsive cockpit layout are restored.
- Broker connections, orders, trade execution, and automatic portfolio mutation remain outside PHOENIX.

## Visual Baseline

Finance is the premium visual reference for subsequent domain work. Its current motion vocabulary includes orbital rotation, scan lines, allocation-ring drawing, real-chart reveal, restrained glow, and status pulses. Reduced-motion behavior remains supported.

## Deferred Cross-Domain Motion Sprint

The following are intentionally deferred until Training, Nutrition, and Calendar share the cockpit system:

- cohesive panel entrance choreography;
- refined hover and focus transitions;
- shared timing, easing, and stagger tokens;
- final motion consistency and performance audit across all domains.

These items must not be implemented as isolated Finance polish. Finance receives no new feature work until the cross-domain design sprint or a correctness regression requires it.
